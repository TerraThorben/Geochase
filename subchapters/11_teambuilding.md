## Teambuilding and Fun activites

Classes in mineral and rock identification are usually at the beginning of a curriculum and the beginning of studying Earth Sciences (or similar) at a university. Consequently, students entered a new educational environment and peer/learning groups have to be founded.
To get to know each other, team building exercises are appreciated at the beginning of a study, while deepening the first knowledge about rocks and minerals.

Here are few ideas to intergrate some fun geoscientific tasks:
