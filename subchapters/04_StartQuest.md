# Starter Questions
## You should start easy and repeat important thing


You should start easy to boost the confidence of the students. I suggest also to include some fundamental questions to repeat the general knowledge to prepare upcoming questions. Here, students can learn something, which they can use later during the trip.


E.g. 
  How do you navigate and orient yourself without a smartphone
Answer: Maps, compass, sun, stars, moos at trees, …  Later: Which building is located north/east… of you?

 Rocks are a assemblage out of …. Minerals indicator colour.
    Later: How do you recognize different minerals in a rock at a first glance?
